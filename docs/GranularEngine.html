Class: GranularEngine

GranularEngine

Granular synthesis TimeEngine implementing the scheduled interface. The grain position (grain onset or center time in the audio buffer) is optionally determined by the engine's currentPosition attribute.

Example that shows a GranularEngine (with a few parameter controls) driven by a Scheduler and a PlayControl: https://rawgit.com/wavesjs/waves-audio/master/examples/granular-engine.html
